Scholarship Programs by TEVTA

The Technical Education and Vocational Training Authority (TEVTA) in Punjab, Pakistan, is dedicated to enhancing the skills of the youth through various educational programs and scholarships. These initiatives aim to equip students with the necessary skills to meet the demands of the modern workforce.

TEVTA's Scholarship Programs

TEVTA offers a range of scholarship opportunities to support students pursuing technical and vocational education. These scholarships are designed to alleviate financial barriers and encourage academic excellence among students.

1. Scholarships for Religious Seminary Students

In a notable initiative, TEVTA announced 400 scholarships for students from religious seminaries in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa. These scholarships cover six-month training courses in various fields, including general electrician, computer operator, solar PV technician, mobile phone repairing, tailoring, and embroidery. The selected candidates receive free training, a monthly stipend, and transportation facilities. Upon completion, they are awarded certificates and essential equipment. Source

2. Scholarships for Position Holders

TEVTA, in collaboration with the Punjab Board of Technical Education (PBTE), offers scholarships to position holders in annual examinations. The scholarship amounts are as follows:

  • 1st Position: Rs. 15,000 per annum
  • 2nd Position: Rs. 12,000 per annum
  • 3rd Position: Rs. 9,000 per annum

These scholarships are awarded based on the results of the annual examinations, and only candidates who pass the entire examination in a single attempt are eligible. Source

3. International Scholarships

TEVTA also facilitates international scholarship opportunities for its students. For instance, 38 TEVTA students were awarded fully funded TANG Scholarships to study in China. These scholarships cover tuition, boarding, and travel expenses, providing students with exposure to international education and skill development. Source

Application Process and Eligibility

Eligibility criteria and application procedures for TEVTA scholarships vary depending on the specific program. Generally, applicants must meet academic requirements, demonstrate financial need, and fulfill other criteria set by TEVTA or collaborating institutions. Detailed information and application forms are available on TEVTA's official website. Source

Conclusion

TEVTA's scholarship programs play a crucial role in promoting technical and vocational education in Punjab. By providing financial assistance and opportunities for skill development, TEVTA empowers students to achieve their educational and professional goals, thereby contributing to the socio-economic development of the region.
